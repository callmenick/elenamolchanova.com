I'm Elena Molchanova, a storyteller
and a maker.

I've worked across several creative platforms, including advertising, motion graphics and video production, visual arts, graphic design and fashion. I have lived in London, Trinidad, Bali, Oakland CA, Santiago and currently reside in Vancouver, BC.

I'm an aspiring author, and I've completed my first novel, Trinidead, in 2018.

Trinidead is a psychological thriller. When an older British expat living on a Caribbean island befriends a charming young couple from London, he is oblivious of the true nature of their relationship, or their deadly mission, which is about to hit close to home.

Presently, I am working on my second novel, Party Done, which is a follow up to Trinidead.

I am currently seeking literary representation. To request the synopsis and sample chapters or a full resume, please email me at me@elenamolchanova.com.
